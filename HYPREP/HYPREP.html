Welcome to the sub-webpage of Hydrocarbon Pollution Remediaton Project (HYPREP)

Hyprep has the mandate to provide alternative livelihood for people of Ogoniland whose sources of income have been deversely affected by oil spills. At HYPREP, our mission is clear: to restore and revitalize communities impacted by hydrocarbon pollution, with a primary focus on the Ogoniland region in Nigeria
